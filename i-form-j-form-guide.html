📄 What is I‑Form? (Form I)

I‑Form is the Kacha Arhtiya Sale Bill issued by a commission agent (arhtiya) to the buyer/trader. It records the purchase of agricultural produce from a farmer and includes all market charges like commission, labour, stitching, and loading. It serves as a primary transaction document and is legally required under Rule 24(12),(13) and 16-F(2)(ix) of the Punjab Agricultural Produce Markets Act.

📋 I‑Form Contents

  • Arhtiya firm name, address, license
  • Date of purchase & commodity details
  • Number of bags, loose kg, total weight (quintals)
  • Rate per quintal & total purchase amount
  • Market charges breakdown: Commission (2.5%), Labour (filling/weighing), Stitching, Loading
  • Sub total (amount + charges)
  • Average rate per bag

⚖️ Why I‑Form Matters

  • Legal record for the buyer and arhtiya
  • Basis for calculating market fees & VAT/GST
  • Proof of transaction in case of dispute
  • Helps in auditing and stock reconciliation

💰 What is J‑Form? (Form J)

J‑Form is the Final Payment Bill given to the farmer. It shows the net amount payable after deducting only the labour charges (unloading/cleaning/filling) from the total sale value. Commission, stitching, loading and other market fees are NOT deducted from the farmer — those are borne by the buyer/trader. J‑Form ensures the farmer receives the correct amount as per mandi norms.

📌 Key difference: I‑Form includes ALL charges (commission + labour + stitching + loading) and is for the buyer. J‑Form deducts ONLY labour charges and shows the farmer's net payment.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🔸 Is I‑Form mandatory for all mandi sales?

Yes, under Punjab Mandi Rules, every Kacha Arhtiya must issue an I‑Form for each purchase lot. It must be preserved for inspection.

🔸 Can I use Arhatiya Calc for Haryana mandis?

Absolutely. The format is identical; you can adjust rates as per your local APMC. Many Haryana arhtiyas use our calculator.

🔸 What if loose kg is more than half a bag?

Our app adds one extra bag equivalent for labour calculation (e.g., 75kg loose with 50kg bag = 1 extra bag labour). This matches real mandi practice.

🔸 Does J‑Form include commission?

No. J‑Form is the farmer's payment bill — only labour deduction is shown. Commission and other charges are borne by the buyer.
